public subroutine subblock(quadblock, factors, positions, curr_weight, spline1, spline2)

Uses

    • mod_global_variables

Builds the quadblock. The four subblocks are calculated based on the specified factors, positions in the block, weights and basis functions. These four subblocks are then added to the quadblock that is passed along, and the filled quadblock is returned.

Note

For a 2x2 block at index (i, j) in the top-left subblock we have (2i, 2j) as index of the bottom-right corner of the 2x2 block. This is referred to as the "main index" of the 2x2 block. The other three corners are then filled by subtracting one from an index. We hence use the following main indices for the four different corners of the quadblock:

  • top-left: (2i, 2j)
  • top-right: (2i, 2j + dim_subblock)
  • bot-left: (2i + dim_subblock, 2j)
  • bot-right: (2i + dim_subblock, 2j + dim_subblock)

Note

The positions array contains the actual positions of the block to fill. For example, the first index could be positions(1, :)=(1, 2) which means that the quantity in factors(1) goes into the 2x2 block at (1, 2).
